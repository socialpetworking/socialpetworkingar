Prices

  • Dog care* $12 per dog
    No need for puppy jail! We will provide care in the comfort of your pup's home. Home check ins include feeding, watering, and potty breaks. Plus, we'll make sure to get in some play time and lots of lovin'!
  • Cat care* $12 per cat
    No need to stress yourself or your kitty out with a carrier and a car ride! We'll be happy to check on your cat while you're away. All home check ins include feeding, watering, and litter box care.
  • Dog walking $15 per pet for 30 minutes
    No time for a walk? No problem! Let us help you get your pup some exercise. You and your dog will both benefit from a walk, because a tired dog is a happy, better behaved dog. Walks are also good for you pup's health, because it helps manage weight gain, and it naturally grinds down their toenails (hooray for fewer nail trims!).
